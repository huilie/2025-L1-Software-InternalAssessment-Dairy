The NewZealand Dairy Products

New Zealand builds a global reputation for “pure, grass-fed, and sustainable” dairy.

At NZ Dairy, we care not only about quality but also about the planet. Our farmers use sustainable practices to protect the land, the animals, and future generations. We understand that healthy land is the cornerstone of producing high-quality dairy products. Therefore, NZ Dairy farmers actively implement measures such as rotational grazing, water resource management, and carbon emission reduction to ensure our farms coexist harmoniously with nature.


Made from grass-fed milk, New Zealand butter is known for its rich flavor and natural golden color.As early as the mid-19th century, New Zealand exported butter to Britain.

Cheddar is the most iconic, offering a mild, consistent taste. In recent years, New Zealand has also developed specialty cheeses like blue cheese and brie.

A cornerstone of New Zealand’s dairy exports, milk powder is easy to store and transport, making it highly demanded worldwide.Milk powder production began in the early 20th century.
